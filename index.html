About

From Hip-Hop to Oldies, we got you covered!

Compact

Gone are the days when record players were stationary pieces of furniture. Now, record players come in all shapes and sizes! From being built into a table, to folding up in a briefcase-like container, there's a record player fit for any room!

Retro

If you're one of those people who like a good nostalgic feeling, records can definitely do that. First introduced in 1948, long play records have continued to grow in popularity.

Modernized

With the growing popularity of records comes the growing changes. With our current technology, we now have record players that are capable of connecting to bluetooth speakers. Now we can listen to our records almost anywhere!
